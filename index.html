Lineage 2 Server FAQ

What is Premium?
Premium increases: - Item enchant success rate - Drop & spoil rates - EXP & SP rates - Craft success rate - Access to special chat using ^
How does the Monastery of Silence instance work?
The Monastery of Silence is a special farming instance. Duration: 1 hour per day Reset: every 12 hours Limit: 1 IP Area type: Combat zone
What does Monastery of Silence drop?
- Huge amount of Adena - Chance for S-grade +0 weapons - Chance for SA weapons
What events are available?
- Raid Boss Event (1 hour) - Squash Event (20 minutes) - Castle vs Castle Event (20 minutes)
What rewards can I get from events?
Raid Boss Event: - Raid boss drops - Special bosses in Pagan area Castle vs Castle: - Event Medals per kill Squash Event: - Pumpkins drop Event Medals
How do Olympiad Games work?
Available on: Thursday / Friday / Saturday Only Olympiad gear is allowed (from player shop)
Can I get + weapons or armor via donation?
No. Only scrolls are available. Enchanting is at your own risk. Exalta armor is only +0.
What is an AIO Buffer?
Provides all buffs including Overlord buffs. Duration: 2 hours (same as normal buffs)
